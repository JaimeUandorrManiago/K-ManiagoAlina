Introduction

Thor Odinson, also known as the God of Thunder is the son of Odin Odinson, King of Asgard, which makes him the Prince. Though from Asgard, he has many connections with people from Earth, being one of the strongest members of the Avengers, wherein he is the only godly being present.

Strengths

--Strength 1--

Thor, being the God of Thunder, is equipped with very useful lightning powers.

--Strength 2--

He is also equipped with a hammer and/or an axe only few are worthy of. They are called the "Mjolnir" and the "Jarnbjorn" respectively.

--Strength 3--

Like Captain America, he is also a natural leader when needed.

Weaknesses

--Weakness 1--

He can take his enemies too lightly and underestimate them at times.

--Weakness 2--

He doesn't negotiate well.

--Weakness 3--

In the earlier days, his temper wasn't as controlled as it is now.

Personality type

ESFJ

He is honest and straightforward, even when he’s not the smartest person in the room, he is always motivated to do what he believes is right.
